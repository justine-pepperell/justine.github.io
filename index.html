About me

Kia ora, I am Justine. I am a full stack product developer based in Pōneke (Wellington).

As a web developer, I focus on simple solutions that support the accessibility of digital products and services. My goal is to create technology that everyone can use to support the aspirations of whānau in all its forms across Aotearoa.

Prior to retraining in web development, I worked in education and health and disability services in positions involving consulting, analysis, business development, and served on the board of Interpreting New Zealand.

My diverse work history and education have provided me with a strong understanding of issues relating to applying technology solutions in health, education, and social services.
